The Automatic Quote Generators (aka AutoPeople)

An Automatic Quote Generator - generates sound bites that almost sound like someone else, and are somtimes funny as hell: AutoFreya, emulating the doubtfully-anonymous-teen Freya AutoLG, for popular blogger Louis Gray AutoMona, for, well, I can't describe who Mona is yet. I haven't figured out. Just someone cool, maybe? AutoAtwood, for one of the few for-the-masses dev coder Jeff Atwood

A few of them (@AutoLG & @AutoMona) also have twiwtter bots for them, posting short tweets once every seven minutes.

And ofcourse, I open sourced the code for all this - and made it available on github :)
